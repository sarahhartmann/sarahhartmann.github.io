Who I am

I'm a 25-year-old UI/UX Designer. My goal is to get a new, challenging UX job at a B2C company.

I have experience working on an agile team for a B2B product. I've also worked on B2C projects and have worked directly with clients in the past.

When I'm not creating intuitive experiences, I love going to concerts, playing video games and board games, and going to breweries.

What I do

For almost two years now, I've been working as a Jr. UI/UX designer. I create and design new features for my company's product and have experience designing for web and mobile. I collaborate with other designers, product managers, developers, and stakeholders on projects.

How I do it

Design Tools
